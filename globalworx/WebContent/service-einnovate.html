Openera

Globalworx is a proud reseller of Openera – one of Canada’s most innovative Canadian SaaS companies. Openera is available as a featured application on our CloudWorkspace or as a stand-alone application to be built into an enterprise or MSP hosted email platform.

Openera started with a tool that automatically saves email attachments to popular cloud storage services (Dropbox, Box, Google Drive, SkyDrive etc.) and lets people search for important documents across their cloud ecosystem. Today, we are helping companies by monitoring email and cloud files for compliancy risk and automatically tagging, classifying and saving those files according to corporate policy. Openera gives companies the insight and control they need over Shadow IT while enabling, securing and simplifying their cloud operations.

Oracle Identity Management Suite

The following is a list of software including definition of the software that is included with Globalwox’s Oracle Identity and Access Management Suite provided via our Partnership with SaskTel. This is offered to customers either in as a Turn-Key (SaaS) monthly service in the cloud environment or for current Fujitsu we can provide external user licenses at vastly discounted rates well below market even for an Oracle Diamond Partner . The Standard Service includes OIAM Suite 11g with the following features for either internal users, external users, or both:

  • Registration – Capture of an individual’s data. This can be accomplished with either an automated or manual process depending on the size of the requirement.
  • Enrollment – This is the mapping of an individual’s registration data to an organization’s applications or entitlements.
  • Authentication – Identification of an individual to specific applications or systems.
  • Audit – The audit is a capture of all transactional data for subsequent verification and validation.
  • Single sign on – Granting access to targeted resources without re-authentication of an individual.
  • Compliance – Ensuring conformance to internal as well as external policies, procedures and standards.
  • Reporting – Presentation and summary of all transactional information in a variety of formats.
  • Password Reset – Self-service or assisted updates and recovery of lost or expired passwords.
  • Provisioning – Propagation of adds, moves, updates and deletion of specific account data into targeted resources or systems the customer specifies./li>

The Identity and Access Management Service provides access to the most current version of OIAM Suite 11g. Frequent changes and updates to the software packages provided by Vendor (Oracle) will result in changes to the Standard Service and additional service descriptions as may be developed from time to time. For the most recent descriptions of these software packages, please consult the following Oracle web page: OIAM Suite 11g
