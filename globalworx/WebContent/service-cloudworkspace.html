Cloud Workspace

Globalworx’s turn-key “Cloud Workspace™” allows business organizations of any size to cost effectively, securely, rapidly, reliably and with flexibility move their business applications, Data and services to the cloud.

By bundling the Cloud Workspace with best of class data centers and Oracle Identity Access Management, Globalworx allows service providers and IT departments to deploy complete workspaces in the cloud, at astonishingly affordable rates. The Cloud Workspace delivers Windows desktops, business and productivity applications (even custom ones) along with a complete back-office system to users anywhere, anytime, on any device. All services are provisioned and managed with ease and flexibility.

Cloud Workspace allows organizations of any size to support mobile, distributed workers, embrace B.Y.O.D. as a corporate strategy and increase responsiveness and service quality, all without compromise to security, performance or control.

Many ISVs use the power of our platform (and other IaaS offerings) to transform their business into a SaaS model without expensive redevelopment efforts, while VARs and service providers leverage Cloud Workspace’s white-label capabilities and API to seamlessly integrate DaaS solutions into their service portfolios and management and provisioning systems.

* For our Canadian partners we proudly offer a 100% Canadian hosted Cloud Workspace via our Partnership with SaskTel.
