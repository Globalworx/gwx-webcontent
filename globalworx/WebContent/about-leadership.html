About Our Leadership

Our Leaders

Rowland Perkins
President and CEO

Rowland has been in the digital data service provider space a long time, founding ebackup Inc, a cloud backup company since 2001 and now branching out into Globalworx which takes it a giant step further; specializing in Cloud Desktop Services (and all things around it to support users) and consulting services to help people transition their businesses to a cloud environment. On top of that, Mr. Perkins has over 40 years of business experience and 25 years of experience with public companies. He is a director of several publicly traded companies; Corvus Gold Inc. since 2010, Oculus Visiontech Inc. since January 2005, Xiana Mining Inc. since 2011 and Strikepoint Gold Inc. since 2011. Mr. Perkins is a graduate of the University of Manitoba with a degree in Economics and views cloud as a true business enabler. If you are wondering how to improve your business processes…. “come to the cloud”.


Stephen Cahill
SVP, North America

Stephen Cahill is an industry leader and proud graduate of St. Mary’s University in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Stephen has over 20 years of global leadership experience ranging from bootstrap start-ups to Fortune 500’s predominantly focusing on technology and communications. Stephen’s strengths include relationship building and developing teams that create value in partnership. Recently this Cloud circuit rider has been speaking to North American enterprise and SMB’s groups regarding the reality, and ultimately the necessity, of embracing cloud technology today.

James Szarka
SVP Technology

James is one of the three founders of Globalworx. As SVP of Technology at Globalworx, James is responsible for all aspects of the data centre operations and the service delivery process. Prior to his role here, he was one of the two original founders and instrumental in the growth and development of ebackup Inc, Western Canada’s largest cloud backup and storage provider (founded in 2001). In his role of CTO at ebackup, James was responsible for all infrastructure, software and digital storage decisions and deployments. He has worked with endless number of customers to ensure that their corporate information is protected and secure. Previous to ebackup, James worked for Care Factor and worked with tape backup solutions and data center build-outs. James graduated with a Degree in Electronic Engineering (CET) from DeVry Institute of Technology in Calgary, Alberta.
