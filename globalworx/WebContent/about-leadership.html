About Our Leadership

Our Leaders

Rowland Perkins
President and CEO

In 2001, Rowland Perkins founded ebackup Inc. a digital cloud data service provider specializing in offsite data backup, remote data storage, data centre management and business continuity planning. In addition to his current role as CEO of ebackup, Mr. Perkins is a Director of several publicly traded companies; Oculus Visiontech (formerly USA Video Interactive Corporation) since January 2005, Strikepoint Gold since 2011, Dorato Resources since 2011, Corvus Gold Inc. since 2010 and was a former Director of International Tower Hill Mines from 2005 to 2010. He is a graduate of the University of Manitoba in Economics and views cloud, quite accurately, as an economic enabler (hence his title of Chief Revenue Officer).


Stephen Cahill
SVP, North America

Stephen Cahill is an industry leader and proud graduate of St. Mary’s University in Halifax, Nova Scotia. Stephen has over 20 years of global leadership experience ranging from bootstrap start-ups to Fortune 500’s predominantly focusing on technology and communications. Stephen’s strengths include relationship building and developing teams that create value in partnership. Recently this Cloud circuit rider has been speaking to North American enterprise and SMB’s groups regarding the reality, and ultimately the necessity, of embracing cloud technology today.

James Travis Szarka
SVP Technology

James Szarka has been instrumental in the growth and development of ebackup, Western Canada’s largest cloud storage provider, from its inception in 2001. In his role of CTO at ebackup, James is responsible for all infrastructure and digital storage. He has worked with endless number of customers to ensure that their corporate information is protected and secure. Previous to ebackup, James worked for Care Factor and worked with tape backup solutions and data center build-outs. James graduated with a Degree in Electronic Engineering (CET) from DeVry Institute of Technology in Calgary, Alberta.
