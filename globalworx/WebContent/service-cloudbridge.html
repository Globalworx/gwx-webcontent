CloudBridge

Globalworx’s “CloudBridge” consulting practice can help you transition your applications to the cloud quickly, efficiently and securely and build a truly strategic cloud operational model – one that will deliver net new innovation, revenue and value differentiation to your organization.

Leveraging a structured migration methodology, we enable a close-to-seamless transfer of applications and data to a fully managed “Cloud Workspace™” environment – one that can be leveraged to drive operational agility and technology commercialization.

  • Private Cloud – Private cloud is cloud infrastructure operated solely for a single organization, whether managed internally or by a third-party and hosted internally or externally.

  • Public Cloud – Public clouds are owned and operated by third-party service providers Customers benefit from economies of scale because infrastructure costs are spread across all users, thus allowing each individual client to operate on a low-cost, “pay-as-you-go” model.
