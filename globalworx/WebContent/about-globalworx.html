About Globalworx

Our Story

The Conference Board of Canada concluded in a 2012 report that Canada’s rapidly declining global competitiveness ranking is a direct result of its weak innovation performance. Our failure to compete globally from an innovation standpoint can be attributed to a number of factors, but perhaps most importantly the absence of an open and democratized cloud ecosystem.

Up until this point in history, Canadian businesses, governments and service providers did not have access to a Canadian hosted open, API driven commercial cloud platform. The landscape changed in 2013 when a team of Canadian cloud experts launched Globalworx’s “CloudWorkspace” – a cloud offering so unique it will transform the Canadian technology industry and allow us to compete globally in the field of cloud computing.
